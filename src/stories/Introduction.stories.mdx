### What is DUBU POT?

In DUBU POT, there is no loser in a game. All the holders will win the prize but there will be a happier winner in every game.

DUBU POT is a simple win-win prize game on Binance Smart Chain.

Deposit crypto into one of our pots and you automatically gain interest on your assets each day. You are also entered into a weekly prize automatically if you simply deposit the crypto. Everyone in each pot is in with a chance of winning a big prize.

So how does it work? Put simply, your deposit is your ticket for the prize draw. Every time you will draw the coupon for the prize, just the same as a gotcha on the street.

Did you get the rarest ticket? You just win the Crumbs of CAKE with Neat DUBU to your wallet. But even if you don't win you can always get your crypto back with added interest with DUBU.

Please note there is a 5-day Fairplay Timelock on your deposit, or payment of a 5% Fairness Fee (or less) if you would like to exit early. Winnings are also subject to the Fairplay Timelock with a Fairness Fee of a maximum of 5%.
